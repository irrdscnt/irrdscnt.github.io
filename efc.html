About EFC.kg (Education for children)

Purpose of creation

The EFC - education for children website was created to make it easier for parents to enroll a child in an educational institution without wasting time, energy and unnecessary paperwork. It also provides information about schools/kindergartens, its location, contact details, reviews and in general will facilitate the process of choosing a school / kindergarten. The site will not allow the parent to skip the stage of enrollment in an educational institution, as well as notify about important news and updates.

Aim of the project

The main goal of the project is to create an informative and convenient platform based on the Spring framework in the Java programming language, which will enable users to easily and quickly apply to educational institution.

Representatives of schools are given the opportunity to register on the platform with the possibility of filling in all the necessary information about the educational institution, as well as the possibility of considering and further accepting or rejecting an application to study at the educational institution they represent

A parent can use search/filters to find the school they want to apply to. Under the description of each school there are reviews from other parents, thanks to which parents will know for sure whether it is worth sending a child to the chosen school or not.

A parent can submit up to 3 applications to different schools at certain points when the application process begins. School representatives then accept or reject the application. After approval / rejection, parents receive an email about their application.
