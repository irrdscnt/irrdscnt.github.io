About MedTech

Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to tell users about the Prime Clinic medical clinic, about the advantages of its mobile application for ease of use, unlike other similar resources in the App Store. The mobile application provides assistance in forming a positive pregnancy experience, maintaining one's usual physical and socio-cultural state

Aim of the project

To prevent stillbirth of children and maternal death during childbirth. A convenient and intuitive interface for making an appointment with a gynecologist and tracking the state of the body through a mobile application. With these features, clients can visually view and trust the clinic itself.

With the help of the website, the administration can monitor the work of doctors, as well as make appointments for patients, which greatly facilitates the work.

The mobile application provides assistance in forming a positive pregnancy experience, maintaining one's usual physical and socio-cultural state during pregnancy, treatment of pregnancy pathology and concomitant diseases.

The website allows doctors to track appointments and store all patient tests in a database to facilitate work.
