About "White Stork"

Purpose

Our website was created so that people could get acquainted with the menu of the restaurant "White Stork" in advance, also learn about the location of the restaurant, contact details.

Aims of the project

The main objectives of our web-site are: creating a convenient, beautiful and intuitive website interface for users, providing users with the opportunity to explore the full menu. Also view the map with the location of our restaurant, contacts of the administration and also social networks of our restaurant.

Users can view all the necessary information about the restaurant including news, contacts, menus, etc.

In the menu section there are sections: first courses, second courses, desserts, wine list, bar.

Users can leave their reservations forms with all the needed information on our website and later managers of restaurant will contact them.
